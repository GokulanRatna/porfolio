HOW TO GET HERE

BY CAR

The nearest car park is on Sowter Road, 0.2km from The Silk Mill, which has 50 spaces. Up to 1/2hr £1; up to 1hr £1.50; up to 2hrs £2.50; up to 3hrs £3.50; up to 4hrs £4.50; up to 12hrs £5. There are two On-Street Dedicated Parking Spaces on Full Street.

BY TRAIN

With a direct train from London to Derby, you can be at any of our museums in less than two hours!

BY BUS

There are frequent and regular bus services into the Derby city centre, operated by Trent Barton and Arriva. Derby bus station is a 10 minute walk away along the River Derwent directly to The Silk Mill.

BY BIKE

The Silk Mill is on cycle routes 6, 54 and 68, part of the Derby City Cycle Map. There is outdoor bicycle parking to the left of the main entrance.

PLAN YOUR VISIT

The Silk Mill is undergoing a process of significant development to create the Museum of Making, due to open in autumn 2020. The building is currently closed to the public.
